Research Projects

Use of Generative AI for Special Education Environments

Identifying key areas where AI-enhanced tools can enhance lesson planning for students with ASD by integrating UDL principles. Exploring the use of AI-generated lesson plans to better support inclusive education and to create a model for AI-assisted lesson planning.

Rec2Tech

Digital Harbor Foundation (DHF) and University of Maryland Baltimore County (UMBC) collaborated on the Rec-to-Tech Project to understand the Role of Rec Center Educators in Creating Maker-based Technology and Computer Science Learning Hubs for Urban Youth (Rec-to-Tech U).

Space For All

In partnership with Digital Harbor, Space for All: Creating Accessible Technology-Rich Makerspaces and Learning Activities for Youth and Young Adults with Autism.

DIY AT

Do It Yourself Assistive Technology program creates 3D printed Assistive Technology (AT) in partnership with the Maryland Technology Assistance Program.
