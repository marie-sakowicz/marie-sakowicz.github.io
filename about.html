About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Human-Centered Computing at the University of Maryland Baltimore County (UMBC). My research journey is driven by a deep passion for making technology accessible and beneficial for all learners, particularly focusing on the use of generative AI for special education.

With a background in educational technology, I bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications. My work primarily focuses on developing and implementing AI-enabled technologies that enhance educational experiences for students with diverse learning needs.

Research Interests

  • AI in Education
  • Special Education Technology
  • Assistive Technology
  • Universal Design for Learning
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Accessible Computing

Current Research

My current research explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and special education, with a particular focus on developing AI-enabled tools that supports creating personalized learning experiences for students with autism spectrum disorder (ASD). This work involves close collaboration with educators, students, and families to ensure that the technology solutions developed are both effective and user-friendly.

Strengths

  • Strong ability to design and execute research that focuses on the intersection of Human-Centered Computing, education, and Artificial Intelligence.
  • Experienced in the use of qualitative research methods, including user studies, focus groups, participatory design, and data analysis.
  • Skilled in setting clear objectives, managing timelines, and aligning team efforts with project goals.
  • Passionate about leveraging Artificial Intelligence to create inclusive, engaging, and personalized learning experiences.
  • Knowledgeable in the application of Universal Design Learning (UDL) principles to support diverse learner needs, including students with disabilities.
  • Focused on ensuring ethical use of AI in education, with a strong emphasis on privacy and data protection.
  • Committed to advancing the integration of AI-driven tools into educational settings to enhance teaching and learning outcomes.
  • Strong collaborator and able to manage interdisciplinary teams and diverse stakeholders, including educators, researchers, and technology developers, to ensure project success.
