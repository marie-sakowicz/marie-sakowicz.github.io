My Ph.D. Journey

I entered the HCC Ph.D. program in the Spring of 2024 after completing two years in the Human-Centered Computing (HCC) M.S. program. I was drawn to the research and wanted to continue my journey as a Ph.D. student. My current research focuses on the use of AI-enabled technology in special education and its impact on accessibility for students with disabilities. I plan to propose early in the fall semester of 2025. This semester, I plan to finalize my committee members and continue analyzing the data collected from my study thus far. Additionally, I will conduct a participatory design study with a special educator to create a lesson generated with AI that is accessible to students with Autism.
