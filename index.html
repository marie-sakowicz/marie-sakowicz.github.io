My Ph.D. Journey

I entered the HCC Ph.D. program in the Spring of 2024 after completing two years in Human-Centered Computing (HCC) M.S. program. I was drawn to the research and wanted to continue my journey as a Ph.D. student. My current research is on the use of AI-enabled technology in special education and the impact on accessibility for students with disabilities.
